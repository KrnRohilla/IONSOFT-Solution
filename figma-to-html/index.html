No Baking, All Fun!

Since 2019, Doughlish® has been creating a stir amongst cookie dough lovers, foodies and those seeking an indulgent treat. It’s the world’s first shelf-stable edible cookie dough product. No refrigeration necessary! Doughlish is made with an egg-free, butter-free formula and heat-treated flour, making it safe to eat and shelf stable for up to 270 days. Doughlish® has continued to grow in popularity, and is now available in grocery stores across the country and add new flavors and sizes
